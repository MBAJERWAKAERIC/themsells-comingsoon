What is Themsells?

Themsells fuses the concept of E-Commerce with Classifieds. Take it as marrying Amazon with Upwork. It lets the buyer post queries regarding the product/service they require and then the corresponding sellers propose the buyer to get the deal. This way buyer gets to determine from whom to buy depending on the seller rating and profile. A seller can be a company, a shopkeeper or a freelancer.

But Why?

Right now, the e-commerce market is dominated by the sellers and the buyers are presented with a new platform/website/app each day to buy from. The principal objective of Themsells is to give control to the buyer. Have buyers determine the best seller in the market and this is also going to break the idea of leading and obvious sellers. New sellers would be able to make their way into the market more easily and the one with the best products and services will be able to get more sales.

And what about the Sellers?

Themsells is going to utilize Escrow payments for maximum security and minimum risk. This way the seller doesn't get paid until the buyer approves, BUT, the buyer has to deposit amount to escrow before the seller mails the product or the freelancer starts working. If the seller has shipped the product and the buyer is still not agreeing to release the payment, the seller can always file a dispute. This way we will interfere between the deals and will resolve the issue. We are going to use cash on delivery payments too, but we are going to let the seller decide whether or not they are comfortable with getting paid through COD. So if they are not, they won't be presented with COD requests.
